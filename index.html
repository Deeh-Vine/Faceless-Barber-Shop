The Faceless Story

Started by a passionate student barber at the University of Ilorin, Faceless Barber Shop was born out of a need for premium, accessible grooming for the modern student and professional.

Our founder believes that your look defines your confidence. By bringing the barbershop experience to you, we eliminate the friction between a busy schedule and a perfect cut.
